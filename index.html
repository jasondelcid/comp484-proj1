Why Visit Japan?

Japan offers a unique blend of tradition, innovation, and unforgettable experiences.

Ancient temples and shrines ~ Futuristic cityscapes ~ World-renowned cuisine

Efficient public transportation ~ Safe and welcoming culture

Travel Basics

Budget

  • Mid-range daily budget: $80–$150
  • Accommodation varies by city and season
  • Cash is still common (especially small shops)

Flights

  • About 10–14 hours from the U.S. West Coast
  • Major airports: Tokyo (HND/NRT), Osaka (KIX)
  • Booking 2–3 months ahead helps with prices

Transportation

  • IC cards (Suica / PASMO) for trains and convenience stores
  • Shinkansen for fast intercity travel
  • Google Maps is extremely reliable for navigation
