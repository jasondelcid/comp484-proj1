Osaka

Friendly vibes, bright streets, and Japan’s food capital.

Phrase: いただきます (let’s eat)

Osaka essentials

Osaka is known for street food, nightlife, and a warm local attitude. Come hungry and explore at night.

Getting around

  • Subway: Easy for getting around the city.
  • Base area: Namba is super convenient.
  • Day trips: Great access to Kyoto and Nara.

Etiquette basics

  • Be mindful in crowded street-food areas.
  • Keep walkways clear when stopping for photos.
  • Carry small cash for food stalls.

Quick tips

  • Go to Dotonbori after dark for the full effect.
  • Try multiple small dishes instead of one big meal.
  • Look for local markets for snacks and souvenirs.

Food to Try in Osaka

Osaka’s famous comfort foods (must-try!).

Takoyaki

  • Octopus balls with sauce and bonito flakes
  • Osaka’s most iconic street snack
  • Best eaten hot, fresh off the grill
