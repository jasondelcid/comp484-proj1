Kyoto

Timeless temples, quiet streets, and Japan’s most iconic traditions.

Phrase: おはよう (good morning)

Kyoto essentials

Kyoto is Japan’s cultural heart. Plan for early mornings, lots of walking, and plenty of time to slow down.

Getting around

  • Buses: Great coverage, but can get crowded.
  • Walking: Many areas are best explored on foot.
  • Rent a bike: A calm way to see neighborhoods.

Etiquette basics

  • Be respectful at shrines and temples.
  • Keep voices low in quiet streets.
  • Follow “no photo” signs carefully.

Quick tips

  • Visit popular spots early to avoid crowds.
  • Bring comfortable shoes—expect lots of walking.
  • Cash helps at smaller tea houses and shops.
